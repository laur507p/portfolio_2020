Space Attack

During 1. semester we learned JavaScript and then had to create a small video game using it. I created the game “Space attack”, inspired by the 1978 game Space Invaders and the Men in Black movies. The goal here is to shoot the aliens and avoid shooting the men in black. I designed the game in a pixel art style and thought the whole ting was very fun to make.

Opia

For our first project of 2. semester at KEA, we created a site that represented a common passion among our group. We chose to create a wine brand from scratch and called it Opia. We designed the bottles, made mockups and created a site for our brand. We also wanted to challenge ourselves by making a split screen that scrolls in different directions, which gave the site a fun twist.

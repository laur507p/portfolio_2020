Askur Magazine

While studying Graphic Media Design at the Technical College in Reykjavík, the final project is making the magazine Askur. Everyone in the class created an individual magazine which was then combined into one. We got to choose our own topics and content for our pages, where I chose to focus on artists and art magazines.
